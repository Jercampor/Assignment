Classification: light scout unit

The Voteless

Classification: light chaff unit

Behavior: Will chase you and try to kill you

tactics: Will run at you mindlessly and swarm you

Weakspots/Weaknesses

  • Completely Unarmored, Vulnerable to all weapons
  • Fire
  • Gas
  • Electricity

The Overseer

Classification: Medium officer unit

Behavior: Will try to chase, shoot and clobber you to death

tactics: Will pull out a shield to cover itself and then rush at you, when far away will fire at you

Weakspots/Weaknesses

  • Medium armor, Vulnerable to all weapons
  • Fire
  • Gas
  • Electricity

The Elevated overseer

Classification: Medium ranged unit

Behavior: Will try to chase and shoot you to death

tactics: Will fly around you firing repeatedly until death

Weakspots/Weaknesses

  • Medium armor, Vulnerable to all weapons
  • Fire
  • Gas
  • Electricity

The Crescent Overseer

Classification: Medium ranged artillery unit

Behavior: Will try to fire on your position

tactics: Will take cover or a long enough distance to fire in arc patterns towards you

Weakspots/Weaknesses

  • Medium armor, Vulnerable to all weapons
  • Fire
  • Gas
  • Electricity

The Fleshmob

Classification: Large tank unit

Behavior: Will try beat you to death

tactics: The fleshmob is a disgusting amalgamation of former citizens, it will charge, slash and attempt to fling you around mindlessly

Weakspots/Weaknesses

  • Unarmored, Vulnerable to all weapons
  • Fire
  • Gas
  • Electricity

The Harvester

Classification: Large tank unit

Behavior: Will shoot you with it's vaporisation beam or arc beams

tactics: When far away it will use it's laser beam to vaporise you, when a helldiver gets close however it will switch to it's electric arcs, instantly killing you if you aren't careful

Weakspots/Weaknesses

  • Medium armor, Vulnerable to Anti-tank and medium pen or higher weapons
  • Fire
  • Gas
  • Electricity
